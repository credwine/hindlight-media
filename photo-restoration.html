Common Questions

Photo restoration FAQs

How much does photo restoration cost?
Restoration is quoted per photo based on the amount of damage and the work involved. Send us a picture of the photo and we'll give you a fast, honest quote. Call or text 706.506.5349.
Do you restore photos for people outside northwest Georgia?
Yes. We're based in northwest Georgia and serve Rome, Cartersville, Calhoun, Dalton and the surrounding areas - and we can also work from a high-quality scan you send us.
What kind of damage can you fix?
We repair rips, tears, creases, water damage, stains, and heavy fading, and we correct color on old and discolored prints. If a photo means something to you, it's worth asking about.
What do we get back?
You get a clean, high-resolution digital file of the restored photo that you can print, share, and keep safe forever. Prints can be arranged on request.
