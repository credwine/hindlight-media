Meet the team

Three people, one mission - content that affects lives and helps businesses grow.

Austin Wiggins

Owner // Director & Editor

Lead director, cinematographer, editor, and visionary - Austin's passion for filmmaking began at age fourteen and hasn't wavered since. An Art Institute of Atlanta alum awarded best-in-show in his graduating class, his career has carried his passion throughout the country and many parts of the world.

Becca Terry

Social Media // Second Shooter

Our social media go-to, second shooter, and general production assistant - caring, thoughtful, and always going above and beyond. Constantly learning and eager to challenge herself, she loves her cats and hiking, documented on her ever-growing Instagram @lifeof_pine.

Austin Terry

Production // Content Innovator

General production assistant, creative ping-ponger, and content innovator - afraid of nothing, he even literally walks through the muck (rolled-up pants and all) when the time calls. His resourcefulness, kindness, heart, and hustle are the perfect recipe for a successful job, time and time again.

And oh yeah - the two Terrys are married, and both of the cats are theirs.
